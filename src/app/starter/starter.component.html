Why should you choose Start Coding Coder?

How do we teach our students?

We believe that the best way to learn how to code is through practice. All courses are highly practical.Throughout the class time, we touch upon topics that are relevant to the course. Then we give the students small homeworks assigments that allows them to practice what was taught in class.

Who are these courses for?



Our intentions are to teach all teens from ages 13 to 18. Regarless of expertice. Level 1 courses are for begginners that have not seen a line of code in their life. However, we also have courses for teens who have prior experience and wish to improve their coding abilities. The only thing that is needed from all students is a passion to learn coding.

Class Size

We value the abilty to pay attention to each and every student. So, we have decided that the perfect class size has a maximum capacity of 6 students. This allows students to collaborate in teams if needed, and the ratio allows the teacher enough time to be attentive to every student.
